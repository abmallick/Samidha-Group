ABOUT THE INNOVATION

Technological and scientific advancements have changed the face of the world in the modern times. However, The awareness of technological advancements and Access to these service is steel in a nascent stage in the rural areas. Lack of infrastructure in the rural areas is a critical factor because of which he rural the population remain untouched by the technological advances.

Mr. Sandilya has tried to address this need of rural areas by harnessing his knowledge of computer science and information technology. He has established an IT Awareness centre in the district of Madhepura. Mr. Sandip Sandilya, under Samidha Group, Conceived the idea of providing a course in computer science to the rural Students at a nominal fee and helps them utilize this computer education as an income generating activity. A Certification course in office automation (COAI) and Internet having 3 months duration Has been design by him and is being offered to the aspiring students. With the successful completion of the basic course, advanced course are offered to the student in the area of database management, programming and integration of front end programming language with backend database. The fee for the course is just Rs. 500/- only.

The Centre in Madhepura Block currently has the facility of 18 computer systems. The Centre has provided free of cost basic hardware training along with personality development training to the students. The centre also conducted Ethical Hacking Workshop for the first time in Kosi Division.Besides face-to-face, Samidha group has also been providing free computer education through internet in Hindi language, and within one year, around 4,32,000 (http://www.samidhafoundation.wordpress.com) students have been benefited by this.

IMPACT

The IT Awareness Centre has mobilized youth, towards computer education and has encouraged them to take it as a mean of increasing employment. Currently 2865 student are enrolled in the Centre and 282 student have successfully taken the job of data entry operator in different districts of Bihar under the E-Governance project. Other student Have stared their own DTP service in the area. A small beginning has generated hope for many in the Area.
